About Dlike

Dlike is a blockchain based dapp (decentralized application) built on top of steem blockchain which offers sharing of content on the blockchain and rewarding its contributors in the form of STEEM cryptocurrency and Dlike tokens. With an earning modal for users, entire focus of Dlike is to build social interaction among community members to keep each other updated through link sharing with latest happening in the world and on top of that we are building a token based economy for the participating community to get rewarded for their efforts to develop a real informative face of internet.

Share What You Like

Dlike give its contributors utmost freedom of expression and allow users to share what they like within the required parameters of useful and informative link sharing for community.

No Censorship

As compared to any other social media platform, Dlike ensures no censorship is being done on platform as link being shared on network is copied to blockchain and is non-editable.

Proof Of Brain Rewards

Being on steem blockchain, Dlike follows the Proof of brain mechanism of its parent blockchain for rewarding its contributors who perform content finsind and sharing activities.

Community Driven Dapp

With a complete beleif in decentralization, Dlike is run by its community managers in the form of moderators and curators who are responsible for ensuring the quality of link sharing.

Dlike Token Economy

Dlike tokens are utility tokens that various participants can hold or consume within the platform, and thus derive their entire value from Dlike Platform.
The most important KSF (Key Successful Factor) of the Dlike platform is to build the real user base and amplify our community activities, rather than just focusing on the future token values. So, right from the very start we we are running a unique way to distribute our tokens - ecosystem and community building bounty programs.
